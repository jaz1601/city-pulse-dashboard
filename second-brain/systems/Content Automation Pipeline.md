# Content Automation Pipeline

## Overview
Automated content generation system for Letterman AI.

## Status
🚧 **BUILD IN PROGRESS** - Started March 16, 2026

## Components

### 1. Content Calendar Generator
- Rotates between: AI tools for non-tech people + Passive income ideas
- Frequency: 3x/week (Mon/Wed/Fri)
- Tone: Friendly, simple, no jargon

### 2. Article Drafter
- Uses Letterman AI `/write-article` command
- Publication: "Yasmine Digital Haven"
- Auto-generates headlines and content

### 3. Review Queue
- Saves drafts for approval
- Morning email delivery
- Simple approve/reject workflow

- **Revenue Potential:** More content = more traffic
- **Efficiency:** Zero manual work until review
